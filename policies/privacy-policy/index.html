    • PURPOSES OF PROCESSING YOUR PERSONAL DATA

    Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. processes personal information to enable it to act as an intermediary for any transactions including DMCA Takedown Notice, and anti-piracy services for its clients; promote its goods and services; maintain its accounts and records and to support and manage its staff.

    Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. may maintain personal data for:

  • clients
  • suppliers
  • professional advisers and consultants
  • enquirers, complainants
  • employees
  • claimants

    • LEGITIMATE INTERESTS FOR PROCESSING YOUR PERSONAL DATA

    Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. recognises the importance of protecting the privacy of the users. Any personal data collected will be treated as confidential in line with the principles of GDPR Article 5.

    What information does Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. collect?

    1. Information given directly to Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. by the data subject.
    2. Information about the data subject given to Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. by a third party.

    Conditions for legally processing personal information

    1. Data subject consent to the processing
    2. Establishing and delivering a contractual obligation for the data subject
    3. Compliance with a legal obligation
    4. To protect the vital interests of the data subject
    5. Performing a task carried out in the public interest or in the exercise of official authority
    6. Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd.’s legitimate business interests

    Condition 2 does not apply to contacts where communication is B2B. Condition 5 does not apply to Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd..

    TYPE OF PERSONAL DATA COLLECTED

    Contacts (Any person who Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. needs to maintain as a contact)
    Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. collects the minimum amount of personal data required to conduct business. Primary contact data includes name, email address, address, and telephone number.

    Staff (permanent, part-time, contract, temporary, non-executive)
    Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. collects information for Human Resources purposes to service the requirements of employees and comply with UK employment and fiscal laws, and regulatory requirements.

    Clients
    To conduct business as an anti-piracy services and consumption data provider, Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. collects only the data it requires. Most of the identification data is at organisation level, but some information collected refers to the Directors: names and qualifications.

    Statutory and non-statutory checks
    To conduct business as an anti-piracy technology and data supplier, and comply with UK law, Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. requests and receives information with regards to data about and related to content which is consumed illegally for the purposes of taking down the infringing content and to collect related data on said infringements.

    Contractual information
    To conduct business, Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. collects only the relevant data it requires to fulfil its contractual obligations. The amount of data collected is the minimum required to meet these obligations.

    Financial
    To collect and effect payments, Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. stores payment and account details for its clients and employees.

    Other
    Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. holds formal and informal events. Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. may collect and industry event attendee contact information and take photographs of the event containing attendees.

    Email addresses
    All email addresses collected by a form or an email message will be stored. Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. will use the email address primarily to respond to requests or conduct correspondence with regards to legitimate interests e.g. a policy, claim, etc. Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. may forward emails to other employees or Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. approved third parties for further processing. Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. may also use email addresses to make contact regarding our organisation or as part of legitimate day-to-day operational needs. Emails and telephone calls will be used for the purposes outlined at the time of collection or registration in accordance with the data subject’s preferences.

    The categories of people who will access or receive the data
    Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. sometimes need to share the personal information it processes with individuals themselves and also with other organisations. Below is a description of the types of organisations Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. may need to share some of the personal information it processes with for one or more reasons.

  • business associates and professional advisers
  • central government
  • courts and tribunals
  • credit reference agencies
  • current, past or prospective employers
  • debt collection and tracing agencies
  • educators and examining bodies
  • employment and recruitment agencies
  • family, associates and representatives of the person whose personal data we are processing
  • financial organisations
  • healthcare and welfare organisations
  • law enforcement and prosecuting authorities
  • ombudsmen and regulatory authorities
  • other companies in the same group
  • suppliers and services providers
  • trade and employer associations

    • TRANSFERRING YOUR PERSONAL DATA

    Your personal data collected by Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. may be stored and processed in the United Kingdom or any other country in which Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. or associated third parties maintain facilities. Should Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. need to transfer your personal data, Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. will take reasonable measures to ensure that transfers of your personal data only go to third parties that also comply with the GDPR.

    KEEPING YOUR PERSONAL DATA

    Retention of specific records may be necessary for one or more of the following reasons:

  • To fulfil statutory or other regulatory requirements.
  • To evidence events/agreements in case of disputes.
  • To meet our operational needs.
  • To meet any historical purposes.

    • Personal data that is collected and subsequently never used for any business purpose will be reviewed and may be destroyed at Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd.’s discretion.

    YOUR RIGHTS OVER YOUR PERSONAL DATA GIVEN TO Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd.

    You have rights regarding the personal data we store on your behalf. These are:

  • Access to a copy of your personal data;
  • Object to processing;
  • Stop receiving direct marketing material;
  • Object to decisions being taken by automated means;
  • Have inaccurate personal data rectified, blocked, erased or destroyed;
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO;
  • Claim compensation for damages caused by a breach of the GDPR.

    • Should you ever wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact the Data Protection Officer.

    WHY Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. NEEDS TO USE YOUR PERSONAL DATA

    Your personal data is required for communication and setting up a contractual agreement to provide products and services. Without this data Ansals Software Pvt. Ltd. will not be able to communicate with you or enter into a contractual agreement with you. This includes both business and employment contracts.
